Mayor’s Opportunity Scholarship





The Mayor’s Opportunity Scholarship launched in 2017 as a partnership between the Executive Office of the Mayor and the Department of Employment Services. The purpose of this scholarship is to provide financial assistance to eligible MBSYEP participants who applied and were selected through a rigorous application process. Since it’s inception, we’ve awarded 227 Mayor’s Opportunity Scholarships totaling $619,000.


For more information on the Mayor’s Opportunity Scholarship program, please contact mos.oyp@dc.gov.
